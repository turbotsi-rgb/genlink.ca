What We Do

Services

01 — IDENTITY
Enterprise Identity Architecture & Design
End-to-end identity architecture for complex enterprise environments. We design systems that scale, survive audits, and align with Microsoft best practices and Zero Trust principles.
02 — HYBRID
Hybrid Identity (AD DS + Entra ID)
Seamless integration of on-premises Active Directory with Microsoft Entra ID. Entra Connect design, configuration, troubleshooting, and optimization for hybrid environments.
03 — MIGRATION
Forest-to-Forest & Multi-Forest Migrations
Complex Active Directory migrations driven by M&A, restructuring, or consolidation. We handle trust relationships, SID history, object migration, and cutover planning.
04 — SECURITY
Conditional Access & Zero Trust
Conditional Access policy design, MFA rollout and enforcement, and Zero Trust baseline implementation. Security that doesn't get in the way of the business.
05 — GOVERNANCE
Identity Governance & Lifecycle Automation
HR-driven provisioning and deprovisioning workflows, joiner-mover-leaver automation, and identity lifecycle management aligned to organizational process.
06 — MESSAGING
Exchange Online Migrations
Cutover, staged, hybrid, and cross-tenant Exchange migrations. Hybrid configuration, remediation, and post-migration support to ensure clean, reliable transitions.
07 — CLOUD
Azure & M365 Migration Planning
Cloud migration planning that aligns identity, messaging, and security foundations before you move. Structured, risk-aware, and built around your operational reality.
08 — INFRASTRUCTURE
PKI Design & Implementation
Certificate authority design, PKI hierarchy planning, and implementation for enterprise environments requiring strong authentication and code-signing infrastructure.
09 — ACCESS
PAM & VPN Modernization
Identity components of privileged access management and VPN modernization initiatives. We integrate identity controls with broader security architecture programs.
